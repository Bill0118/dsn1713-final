Our Story

Golden Capital, a Cantonese style Chinese food restaurant has been serving up authentic dishes for over two decades. The restaurant is renowned for its mouthwatering dim sum, where steaming bamboo baskets filled with dumplings and buns are wheeled around on carts, enticing diners with their aroma. With vibrant red and gold decor, the warm atmosphere welcomes families and food lovers alike. Signature dishes like Peking duck and sweet and sour pork are complemented by an extensive tea menu, ensuring a perfect pairing for every meal. Each Sunday, the restaurant hosts a bustling brunch, drawing in crowds eager to savour the flavours of traditional Cantonese cuisine.
